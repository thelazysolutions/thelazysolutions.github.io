SMASH

Pursue classes are designed to develop performance, coordination, and agility through obstacle-specific movements in preparation to tackle any OCR skillfully! Each class is scalable for all abilities, which means anyone can join in on the FUN.

Build up your running miles with this class. Following a programme to improve your running endurance, this class is perfect for both OCR training and those who just love to run.
